Overview

  • Parsed date range: 2000-01-03 to 2022-12-30.
  • Transactions table: A2_EDA_Residential.csv has 49,301 rows × 68 columns.
  • ZIP → neighborhood mapping via Boston_Neighborhoods_Zipcodes.csv (missing labels: 0.25% of rows).
  • Price outliers / placeholders removed to avoid distorting distribution plots.
  • Flip horizons missing for most rows (66.95%) because a linked prior purchase is required.
  • 23 negative flip horizons excluded as invalid.
  • Coordinates mostly present but not always plausible (e.g., 456 points outside a Boston-ish bounding box).

Sanity check (early 2000s vs recent years)

Cleaned dataset, comparing 2000–2004 average vs 2018–2022 average.

In the cleaned sample, later years show higher investor/entity presence and higher short-horizon resale incidence. Profitability is not directly observed because the dataset does not provide a complete prior purchase price field.
